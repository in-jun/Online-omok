Game Information

Enjoy Gomoku in real-time with various players and acquire new strategies.

Key Features

  • Real-time Play: Enjoy Gomoku battles in real-time with other players.
  • Easy Access: Join the game easily without complicated procedures.
  • Auto-Matching: Upon connecting to the server, get matched automatically for quick battles with other players.

How to Use

  1. Go to the Page: Open your web browser and go to the page.
  2. Connecting to the Server: Upon arrival, you will see a "Connecting to the server" message on the screen. Please wait for a moment.
  3. Matching players: Once the server connection is complete, you'll see a "Matching players" message. Now, wait for matching with other players.
  4. Game Start: When the matching is successful, place stones on the board to start the game. Each player can place stones of their color on their turn.
  5. Utilize Time Wisely: If you don't place a stone within the given time, it will result in a timeout. Make decisions quickly and accurately!

Game Objective

The goal of the game is to place five stones in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, thereby overpowering your opponent.

Players take turns placing stones, and the first to create a sequence of five connected stones wins. The game unfolds on a 15x15 Gomoku board, so challenge your opponent while blocking their moves!
